The Crotch/Jockey/Remote Throttle System

The Crotch Throttle is a motorcycle accessory that allows riders to control the throttle of their motorcycle remotely. It is designed to be used in conjunction with the traditional hand twisted throttle, giving the rider more control options and achieve more comfortable riding positions.

Features

  • Ergonomic design for comfortable use
  • Easy to install on most motorcycle models
  • Durable construction for long-lasting use

Benefits

  • Reduces hand fatigue during long rides
  • Gives the rider more control options over the motorcycle
  • Can help in long rides in cramped positions inherent to riding certain types of motorcycles
